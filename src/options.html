Browsing History Graph Settings

Enter domains you want to track, one per line. For example:
wikipedia.org
reddit.com
mozilla.org

Note: Adding a domain (e.g., "wikipedia.org") will also track all its subdomains (e.g., "en.wikipedia.org", "fr.wikipedia.org"). You don't need to list each subdomain separately.

External Links: The extension will also track navigation to unregistered external sites when you navigate from or to a tracked site. This allows you to see the complete browsing graph.
